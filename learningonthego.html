Overview
Mobile app to learn UX skills on-the-go. Main objective is to leverage simplicity and accessibility for continuous usability and completion and the biggest challenge was ensuring the app offers continuous learning and is designed around the learners’ needs - next-gen learning.
Challenge
  • Students can learn UX/UI Design only by PC
  • Learning alone is difficula with too many sources on net
  • No motivation without gamefication, sharing an knowledge or success
  • Ther’s no such an mobile app in North Macedonia
  • Brainster Academy wants to keep their student after thris UX/UI Design bootcamp
The Solution
  • Can learn on your mobile wherevare you are.
  • Well strucurted program of learning the whole process of UX/UI
  • Motivation with gamefication and share your results with friends.
Project Goal
  • The scope of the project is for the product to support 1 role: End-user - learners
  • This product, once launched, will ultimately serve Brainster as an additional channel to convert users to our UX/UI Design bootcamp, so users could properly go through the full Brainster learning experience.
  • Every week there will be a certain topic for the user to complete. They would have one ‘lesson’ that would take 5-10 minutes. #MicroLearning
Research
I started with a questionary of 6 participants at age of 28-33 years, who arechangeing their careers. Asking how many time they have per day to learn something new about UX/UI Design
40% 10-30 min per day
60% don’t want to share the results on social media
100% motivated with progress bar
66,7% learning with watching videos
For the target group of High Schools students (Senior preferred) I used the persona that was given to me.

Overview of the App
Four Choises of the Learning Process
The user can choose how much time will learn per day at wich leveland she/he will go through the whole process withchossen Hero. The user can choose between some of the instrucotrs of the UX/UI Design Academy of Brainster, who will be hers/his Hero like “virtual instructor”.
Homepage
Clean and Simple
I strive to keep the clean and simple homepage, so the user can easily find the module that should learn and informationabout the duration and lessons that are in.
The user can learn one module per day, so if she/he missed the lesson she/he can learn at the end of the week.
The user can also open the module that she/her passed once.
Lessons
Videos, Q&A, Making Personas, Skatch Wireframes, True or False, UI Design
Тhe lessons are composed of short videos, Q&A, making personas, sketch wireframer, questions with true or false.The users can learn on easy and fun way, from anywerhe at any time of the day.
Gamefication
Motivation and sharing with friends while learning
On every correct answer the users get some motivation sentence and poens- pixels. When they finish the module they get 5 lifes, 20px and a badge. They collect their pixels and can see their position on a liderboard. Also the users can share their results with their friends on a social media.
Profile
Clean and Simple
The users can edit their profile picture, their e-mail or their goal. They can see how many days they’ve learned, how meny lifes they have and at which plase they are on a leaderboard.
